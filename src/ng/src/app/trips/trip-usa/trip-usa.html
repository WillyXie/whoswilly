USA Travel Route

An interactive map visualization of my travel routes, stops, and itineraries across the USA. All coordinate and location information is parsed dynamically from a Google My Maps KML export.

💡 Map Tips

  • Zoom & Pan: Use your mouse scroll wheel or double-click to zoom, and drag to pan across different states.
  • Click Highlights: Click on any blue map pin to see specific descriptions of things done at that stop.
  • Route Info: Click on the dashed lines to view transit paths or driving routes.
